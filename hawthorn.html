want to make a cup of tea?

Collect flowers when in full bloom and dry them. Use 2 teaspoons of blossom per cup and let it brew for 20 minutes.The rest of the flowers can be stored in a jar. Hawthorn blossom is known to have beneficial effects on the heart and circulation.

In Celtic culture, Hawthorn is associated with fertility and the seasonal festival of Beltane (May 1st). In earlier times, Hawthorn was used to form a May Pole, while the leaves and flowers were used to make May Day garlands. It celebrates the onset of summer.
